Choice Helper AAC

A simple visual communication tool for making big choices.

Design Philosophy

Built with a "pocket-sized" AAC experience in mind, Choice Helper focuses on reducing cognitive load for children:

  • Autism-Friendly: Uses a soft color palette (Teal and Coral) to prevent overstimulation.
  • Clear Interaction: Features large visual targets and consistent haptic and audio feedback.
  • Simple Choice Boards: Allows caregivers to quickly set up two to four options with clear labels.

Smart Library & Caching

The app employs a "Triple-Check" strategy to ensure you always have access to the right symbols:

Teal Border Standard Library (102 icons): Core curated icons for food, feelings, and daily activities.

Indigo Border Extended Library (748 icons): Includes Dolch Sight Words (Pre-primer to 3rd Grade) and core AAC vocabulary.

Gray Border Online Search: Real-time access to 28,000+ additional ARASAAC icons, which are auto-cached for future offline use.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Choice Helper AAC is designed for personal use with zero data tracking:

  • Local Storage: Your custom images and choice settings are stored only on your device.
  • No Tracking: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.
  • Offline Capability: The app functions entirely offline, ensuring privacy and reliability.
